ASRJC Study and Fitness Planner

Welcome to the Study and Fitness Planner app! Use the links below to plan your study sessions, fitness routines, food shopping lists, and calculate your BMI. This would help ASRJC students to become responsible and disciplined learners.

  • We included a Study Planner and a Fitness Planner. We aim to encourage students to seek a balance between studying and exercising, as they are responsible for both mental and physical wellbeing.
  • We included a BMI calculator and a Food Shopping Form. This helps students to plan their own diet, based on their BMI class.
  • Do try out our Health Survey below. It can help us to come up with new ideas to help students' fitness.
  • We also encourage ASR students to sign up for our gym membership, which can be beneficial for their health.
